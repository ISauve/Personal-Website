This is an extension I wrote that takes two words of your choice and switches them in your browser. For example, you could tell it to replace every occurrence of 'Justin Trudeau' with 'Prime Minister Hottie'. Reading CBC articles is suddenly much more interesting!
How to run
- Download the file
- Open (unzip) the downloaded file
- Go to chrome://extensions/
- Check 'Developer Mode'
- Click 'Load unpacked extension...'
- Open the downloaded file
- Click on the icon next to your address bar to use!
